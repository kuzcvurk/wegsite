Some exciting news! Tomorrow I will be participating in my first book fair. It’s taking place in the Omni Hotel in Charlottesville, VA. I have a table and copies of my book and business cards and everything. It’s almost like I’m a professional!


Sean has produced the first version of my cover, as you can see to the left. We’re sticking with the sphere and wireframe against a starry background theme. The finished version will be very similar. The “Fall 2015” banner will be gone. And there will be a back side. But otherwise, it’s basically the same. Maybe some tweaking of the lights. Maybe not. Probably not. I mean, you can tell it’s a hand, right? Maybe.


In other exciting news, I left my book on the shelf of a local coffeehouse and someone has taken it. I hope to read it, and not use it for kindling or something. And person, if you’ve found your way to this page from the link on the back cover, Hi! And if you enjoyed it, please leave me a review on Amazon: http://www.amazon.com/dp/B00RM1MMDS   And if you hated it, I’m sorry, and the next book will be better. I promise. It made me cry. For good reasons.


Speaking of that. The first draft is out for feedback! Work is moving along, so I’m hopeful that Fall 2015 banner will not be a lie.
