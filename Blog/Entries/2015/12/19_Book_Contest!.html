”The Children of Doctor Lancing” is finally published! Special thanks to everyone who helped it along.


Are you interested in having a character in the next book named after you? Here’s how to be entered in a random drawing for your name to be used:


Between now and May, when you write a review of either of my books to post on Amazon, email me a copy of your review before you submit it. Along with your review, let me know how you would like your name to appear in print. That’s it!


The longer your review, the higher the chances are that your name will get drawn. So try to be more specific than just saying “I loved it!”


Remember, you can write a review for either “The Sphere” or “The Children of Doctor Lancing”, and if you write one for each, your chances of being picked are even greater. Make sure you email it to me before it goes live, so I know for sure that it’s your review. Good luck!
