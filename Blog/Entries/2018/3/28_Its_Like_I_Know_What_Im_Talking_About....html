Michelle McBeth

 
 
 
 
 


This past Saturday was the monthly meeting of the Northern Virginia chapter of the Virginia Writers Club. I am the Membership Chair of our local chapter, which means I get to welcome new folks to the club and choose the snacks we get for our meetings. Let me just say, the snacks have been awesome.


At the last minute the president asked me to talk about publishing on Amazon. Had I more time to organize I would’ve brought my laptop and walked people through the process of publishing my third book: “The Lunar Conspiracy” since I was in the midst of doing that. As it was, I had a couple copies of my books and the anthology I was published in for people to see what sort of quality you get from CreateSpace. I walked them through the rough process of getting a print book published and answered a bunch of questions about the logistics/cost/etc.


I thought it was a little too half-assed, but people seemed pleased to learn things, even the one guy who had already published on CreateSpace a few years ago.


When it comes down to it, publishing on CreateSpace is really quite easy. There are enough walk-throughs and templates for anyone to do it in short order. The hardest part is just WRITING YOUR BOOK! So get to it.
