Michelle McBeth

 
 
 
 
 


Two bits of exciting news from the short story front. Firstly, my short story “Fire Drill in Motherhood” was selected for inclusion in the Virginia Writers Club’s Centennial Anthology! It’s a story about a woman who unexpectedly gives birth to a dragon instead of a human baby, and the resulting adventures in trying to raise such a child.


Another short story, “The Usual”, won my local chapter Golden Nib contest and is a finalist for the statewide contest, also through the Virginia Writers Club. It’s about an unusual usual customer at a coffee house. Winners announced in November!
